There are many ways to purchase credits on Habbo!

Buy a code with credit card or PayPal

Habbo codes bought on GameTimeZone and PCGameSupply are valid in all Habbo hotels! You can buy one with a credit card or with PayPal. Once you have the code, come back to this page and enter the code in the voucher code field. (Remember to log in first.)

   

Gift cards

If you have a gift card from Subway, CVS/pharmacy, Burger King, Dollar General, Shell, Circle K or oBucks, you can use it to buy everything available on the HabboMall front page - not only credits, but Habbo Club, Builders Club and bundles too!
