There are many ways to purchase credits on Habbo!

Buy a Habbo Prepaid Card

Topping up your credits with a prepaid card is super quick and easy!

  1. Go to your nearest EB Games store. You can find them all over the country. Locate your nearest EB Games store here!

  2. Choose a value:

    • AU$10: 90 Credits
    • AU$20: 190 Credits

  3. Make sure the cashier activates the card as you pay, otherwise the code will not work.

  4. Come back to this page and enter the code (it's underneath the scratch surface on the back of the card) in the voucher code field! (Remember to log in first.)

Buy a code with a credit card or PayPal

Habbo codes bought on GameTimeZone and PCGameSupply are valid in all Habbo country hotels! You can buy one with a credit card or with PayPal. Once you have the code, come back to this page and enter the code in the voucher code field. (Remember to log in first.)

   

Paysafecard

If you have a Paysafecard, you can use it to buy everything available on the HabboMall front page - not only credits, but Habbo Club, Builders Club and bundles too! You can get yourself an account by going to www.paysafecard.com.
